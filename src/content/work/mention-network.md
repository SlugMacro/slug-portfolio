AI Visibility & Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) platform helping brands track and optimize how they appear in AI-generated answers across ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, and other models. Designed the product for collecting, structuring, and surfacing AI mention data — turning raw interaction signals into actionable visibility insights.

## Challenge

Brands had no way to track how they appeared in AI-generated answers. Unlike traditional SEO, there were no tools to measure visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok.

## Approach

Designed the AI mention data pipeline — collecting frequency, context, and sentiment across models. Built Visibility dashboard with brand ranking, visibility share, and model-level breakdown.

## Outcome

Standardized how brands measure and optimize their presence in AI-generated answers — a new category with no prior tooling.
